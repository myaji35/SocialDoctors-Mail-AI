## Workflow Steps

### 1. Service Selection

Identify which service(s) to promote:

**Service Discovery:**
- "Which of our 10 SaaS services would you like to promote?"
- Display available services from `../knowledge/services/`
- Option to promote multiple services as package
- Consider "Hero Product" strategy

**If service info missing:**
- Guide user to input service details
- Save to knowledge base for future use
- Structure: name, description, key features, target audience, pricing

### 2. Target Audience Selection

Define who this promo targets:

**Persona Options:**
- Marketers & Influencers (partnership focus)
- Small Business Owners (efficiency focus)
- Startup Founders (growth focus)
- Industry-specific (agriculture, lifestyle, etc.)
- Custom persona

**Audience Deep-Dive:**
- What's their primary pain point?
- What outcome do they desire?
- What objections might they have?
- What would make them act now?

### 3. Apply "ë¹„ì¦ˆë‹ˆìŠ¤ í´ë¦¬ë‹‰" Framework

Structure content using the clinic metaphor:

**Step 1: Symptom Recognition**
- Identify relatable business problem
- Make it specific and painful
- Use "Do you experience..." hook

**Step 2: Diagnosis**
- Explain why this problem occurs
- Validate the struggle
- Position as common issue

**Step 3: Prescription**
- Introduce SocialDoctors service as solution
- Explain how it addresses root cause
- Use doctor/treatment metaphor naturally

**Step 4: Prescription Details**
- Service features that solve problem
- Easy implementation (like taking medicine)
- Expected results and timeline

**Step 5: Healthy Outcome**
- Paint picture of life after solution
- Quantify benefits when possible
- Social proof or testimonials

### 4. Content Strategy Selection

Choose promotional approach:

**Strategy Options:**

**A) Problem-Solution Story**
- Lead with pain point
- Journey from struggle to success
- Service as the turning point

**B) Feature-Benefit Showcase**
- Highlight 3-5 key features
- Transform features into tangible benefits
- Visual infographic style

**C) Customer Success Story**
- Real or composite case study
- Before/after transformation
- Specific results achieved

**D) Comparison/Alternative**
- "Current way" vs "SocialDoctors way"
- Time/cost savings visualization
- Ease of use emphasis

**E) Hero Product Spotlight**
- Deep dive into killer feature
- Why this changes the game
- Limited-time focus or offer

**Each variation includes:**
- Attention hook (question, stat, or bold claim)
- Business Clinic narrative structure
- Clear value proposition
- Compelling CTA
- Platform-appropriate length
- Strategic hashtags

**Variation Differentiation:**
- Different pain point emphasis
- Alternative metaphors/analogies
- Varied tones (urgent vs educational vs inspiring)
- Different customer personas addressed
- A/B testing potential

### 6. Multi-Platform Optimization

Adapt content for different platforms:

**Facebook Version:**
- Longer storytelling format
- More context and education
- Community engagement focus

**Instagram Version:**
- Shorter, punchier copy
- Visual-first approach
- Story series option

**Twitter/X Thread:**
- Break into tweet series
- Hook in first tweet
- Thread with details
- Final CTA tweet

**LinkedIn Version:**
- Professional tone
- Business case focus
- Industry insights

**Blog Post:**
- Expanded with SEO keywords
- More comprehensive coverage
- Multiple CTAs throughout

- Slide 6-7: Feature breakdown

**Video Script:**

- 60-second explainer
- Customer testimonial clip

### 8. Service Combination Strategy

If promoting multiple services:

**Package Positioning:**
- "Complete Business Health Package"
- "Starter Kit" vs "Pro Clinic"
- Complementary service bundles

**Cross-Promotion:**
- Lead with Hero Product
- Introduce related services naturally
- "Patients who used X also benefited from Y"

### 9. Offer & Urgency Integration

Add promotional elements (if applicable):

**Offer Types:**
- Free trial period
- Discount code
- Early adopter benefits
- Bundle pricing
- Referral rewards

**Urgency Tactics:**
- Time-limited offer
- Limited spots available
- Seasonal relevance
- Problem urgency amplification

**Ethical Considerations:**
- Real scarcity only
- No false pressure
- Transparent terms

**Expected Performance:**

- Optimal posting time

- Estimated reach based on past data

**Recommendations:**
- Testing strategy (which variations to try)
- Budget allocation if paid promotion

- Performance benchmarks (to update later)
