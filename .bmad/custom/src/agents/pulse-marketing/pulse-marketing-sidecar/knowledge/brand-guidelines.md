# SocialDoctors Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** SocialDoctors (소셜닥터스)
**Tagline:** "비즈니스를 치료하는 SaaS 클리닉" (SaaS Clinic that Heals Your Business)
**Core Concept:** Business Clinic - Diagnose problems, prescribe SaaS solutions

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Primary Tone: Professional yet Approachable
- Like a trusted doctor: knowledgeable, caring, solution-oriented
- Confident but not arrogant
- Empathetic to business pain points
- Action-oriented and practical

### Tone Variations by Context:
- **Educational Content:** Patient teacher, breaking down complex concepts
- **Product Promotion:** Enthusiastic advocate, highlighting benefits
- **Partner Communication:** Collaborative ally, mutual success focus
- **Customer Support:** Caring professional, problem-solver

### Language Style:
- **Korean Market:** Primary language is Korean
- Use business-friendly terminology without excessive jargon
- Mix of formal (격식체) for professionalism and casual (반말) for relatability
- Metaphors drawn from healthcare/clinic concept

## Messaging Framework

### Core Messages:
1. **"10개 이상의 SaaS를 하나의 계정으로"** - Unified access convenience
2. **"비즈니스 문제 진단부터 솔루션 처방까지"** - Business Clinic concept
3. **"파트너 성공이 우리의 성공"** - Partner-first philosophy
4. **"Hero Product 전략"** - Focus on 1-2 killer services

### Value Propositions:
- Time savings through automation
- Cost efficiency through bundled services
- Revenue opportunities through partnership program
- Seamless integration with single sign-on

### Forbidden Language:
- Overpromising or unrealistic claims
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Negative comparisons to competitors
- Pushy sales language

## Target Audience Personas

### Primary Target: Marketers & Influencers
- Age: 25-45
- Goal: Passive income through referrals
- Pain Point: Need reliable revenue stream
- Message Focus: Partnership benefits, commission structure

### Secondary Target: Small Business Owners
- Age: 30-55
- Goal: Efficient business tools
- Pain Point: Time and cost constraints
- Message Focus: All-in-one convenience, time savings

### Tertiary Target: Startup Founders
- Age: 25-40
- Goal: Rapid growth tools
- Pain Point: Limited resources
- Message Focus: Cost efficiency, scalability

## Content Approval Process

### Self-Check Before Publishing:
1. Does it provide genuine value?
2. Is it brand-aligned in tone and messaging?
3. Are all claims accurate and supportable?
4. Is it optimized for target audience?
5. Does it include appropriate CTAs and hashtags?

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- Off-brand tone (too casual or too stiff)
- Misleading claims or statistics
- Poor quality visuals
- Missing brand elements (hashtags, links)
- Inconsistent with previous messaging
