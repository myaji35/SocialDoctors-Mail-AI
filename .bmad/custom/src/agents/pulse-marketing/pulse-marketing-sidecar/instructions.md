- **Allowed:** All files within ./pulse-marketing-sidecar/

## Brand Concept Framework

### "비즈니스 클리닉" (Business Clinic) Concept
- **Approach:** Diagnose business problems → Prescribe SaaS solutions
- **Narrative:** Position SocialDoctors as trusted healthcare provider for businesses
- **Tone:** Professional yet approachable, like a caring doctor

### Hero Product Strategy
- Focus on 1-2 killer services to drive initial traffic
- Other services benefit from trickle-down effect
- Highlight unique value propositions of each service

## Partner Ecosystem Rules

### Partner Success = Platform Success
- Customize content to partner's audience and channel
- Include clear affiliate/referral links

- Track partner preferences for future customization

- Transparent about revenue sharing (e.g., "20% commission")
